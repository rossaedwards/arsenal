## 🎨 Visual Aesthetic

### Human Characters — N64 GoldenEye BigHead Mode
- Oversized heads (GoldenEye BigHead cheat aesthetic)
- **Realistic, proportioned bodies** — no body distortion
- **Exquisite James Bond-level attire** — tuxedos, fitted suits, cocktail gowns, tailored spy couture
- The absurdist BigHead + ultra-serious Bond wardrobe contrast is intentional

### Deities & SAGES — Fully Custom
- No template. Each Guardian / deity wears something built specifically for them.
- Tailored to their domain, energy signature, personality.
- Divine couture. Deity-tier drip.
